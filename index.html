How To Use This Plan

  • Easy runs must be genuinely easy — if you can't hold a full conversation, slow down.
  • Strength (Tue/Thu mornings) is locked in. Running sessions are built around it — never add a hard run on strength days.
  • Friday's 7 km loop with friends is your speed/technical day. Descriptions show how to use the route each week.
  • Recovery weeks (Wks 4, 8, 12) are not optional — they're where fitness is absorbed. Run them slow.
  • From Week 8, practise race nutrition (gels/chews) on all long runs so your gut is trained by race day.
  • Optional tune-up race: a half marathon in Week 9 or 10 is ideal. Race it at goal half pace (1:43–1:45) to gauge progress.
  • Taper madness is real — Weeks 16–17 you will feel sluggish. That is normal. Trust the fitness you've built.
