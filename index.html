How This Plan Works

  • 3 running days only (Wed, Fri, Sat). Monday and Sunday are full rest — no exceptions.
  • Long runs alternate hard / step-back every week. A big Saturday is always followed by a lighter Saturday the next week. No two consecutive big runs.
  • Friday is deliberately easy before every long run over 22 km — this is intentional, not lazy. Save the legs for Saturday.
  • All quality work (tempo, intervals, MP blocks) happens on Wednesday, mid-week, far from the long run.
  • Strength training (Tue/Thu) stays. In taper weeks, reduce load but keep the sessions.
  • From Week 8 onwards, practise race nutrition (gels/chews) on every long run over 90 min.
  • Optional tune-up half marathon: Week 7 (step-back week, long run is only 20 km). Race it at ~1:43–1:45 to gauge fitness.
  • Taper madness is real — Weeks 15–17 you will feel sluggish and restless. That is adaptation happening. Trust it.
